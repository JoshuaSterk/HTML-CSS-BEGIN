Polar Bears look innocent, but they are actually the strongest bear spieces on the world.

The white bear, more commonly known as the polar bear, is the largest, heaviest and strongest of all bears. The polar bear is a carnivore. It mainly feeds on the fat of seals, but also eats carcasses of stranded belugas, whales and walruses. They are smart, fast and very strong. A polar bear is very big. When a male polar bear stands on its hind legs, it is about the size of an elephant. Female polar bears are smaller.
